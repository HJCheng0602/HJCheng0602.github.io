## Who Am I

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Intelligence Science and Technology at [Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn/). I'm currently in the early stages of exploring the 3R series (3D Reconstruction, Rendering, and Recognition).

My main focus is on learning the fundamental theories and classic algorithms in this field — from classical geometry-based methods to recent learning-based approaches like DUSt3R, MASt3R, and SLAM3R. I also have a growing interest in ML Systems and infrastructure (CUDA kernels, inference optimization, P/D disaggregation).

## Education

**Peking University** — B.Sc. Intelligence Science and Technology, 2024 – present

## About This Site

This site is a place where I write down what I learn, what I find interesting, and occasional thoughts. Most articles are reading notes on papers in the 3D Reconstruction / Rendering / Recognition space, along with some ML Systems topics. Some posts are in Chinese, some in English.

The name *Two and One* comes from Chapter 42 of the Tao Te Ching:

> 道生一，一生二，二生三，三生万物。

Navigate via the **Blog** menu above to browse all posts, or use the sidebar for recent entries, archives, and tags.
