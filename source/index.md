## Who Am I

I am an undergraduate student majoring in Intelligence Science and Technology at [Peking University](https://www.pku.edu.cn/). I started out in Computer Vision — 3D reconstruction, rendering, and recognition — before shifting my focus fully to ML Systems. These days I care more about what happens under the hood: CUDA kernels, inference optimization, distributed training, and P/D disaggregation.

## Education

**Peking University** — B.Sc. Intelligence Science and Technology, 2024 – present

## About This Site

This site is a place where I write down what I learn, what I find interesting, and occasional thoughts. Posts are mostly reading notes on ML Systems papers and topics. Some posts are in Chinese, some in English.

The name *Two and One* was picked at random when setting up the site. No deep meaning.

Navigate via the **Blog** menu above to browse all posts, or use the sidebar for recent entries, archives, and tags.
